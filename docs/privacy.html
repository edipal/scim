Privacy Policy

Last Updated: 09 March 2026

1. What data we collect

To provide you with access to our application, we collect the following minimal information:

  • Account Data: Your email address, name (if provided via social login), and a unique authentication ID.
  • Security & Network Data: Your IP address, browser type, and login timestamps.
  • App Data: Data you actively create or input while using the application.

2. How we use your data

We use your data strictly to provide, secure, and maintain the service. We do not sell your personal data to third parties.

3. Third-party Sub-processors

We use trusted third-party infrastructure to operate this service securely. By using this service, you acknowledge that your data is processed by:

  • Auth0 (Okta): Acts as our identity provider. They securely process your login credentials and profile information.
  • Cloudflare: Acts as our network security gateway. They process IP addresses and network requests to block malicious traffic and authorize your session.

4. Cookies

We use strictly necessary cookies provided by Auth0 and Cloudflare solely to keep you logged in and secure your session. We do not use tracking or advertising cookies.

5. Your Rights & Deletion

You have the right to request access to, correction of, or deletion of your personal data. To export your data or delete your account entirely from our systems and our identity providers, please contact us at privacy@scimsandbox.net.
